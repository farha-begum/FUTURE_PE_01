This file contains structured AI prompts used to generate website content for a cafe business.

## 🏠 Homepage Prompt

You are an expert website copywriter.

Generate homepage content for a cafe business with the following details:

Business Name: Urban Cafreash  
Locations: Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Kolkata  
Services: Coffee, pastries, snacks  
Target Audience: Students, professionals, casual meetups  
Tone: Modern, friendly, welcoming  

Include:
- Headline (clear value proposition)
- Subheadline
- About Us section (short and engaging)
- Why Choose Us (3–5 bullet points)
- Call-to-Action (CTA)

Make the content simple, engaging, and website-ready.
